What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn shoes in their original packaging. Simply contact our customer service team to initiate a return, and we'll provide you with a prepaid shipping label. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return.

How do I find my correct shoe size?

+

We provide a detailed size chart on each product page. We recommend measuring your foot length and comparing it to our size guide. If you're between sizes, we suggest ordering the larger size. You can also check customer reviews for sizing feedback on specific styles.

How long does shipping take?

+

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 business days) and overnight shipping options are also available at checkout. International shipping times vary by destination, typically ranging from 10-21 business days.

Do you offer free shipping?

+

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $75. For orders under $75, a flat shipping fee of $8.99 applies. We also run special promotions throughout the year with free shipping on all orders.

Are your shoes true to size?

+

Most of our shoes fit true to size, but this can vary by brand and style. Each product page includes specific sizing information and customer reviews that often mention fit. Our customer service team is also available to help with sizing questions.

How do I care for my shoes?

+

Care instructions vary by material. Leather shoes should be cleaned with a damp cloth and conditioned regularly. Suede requires a special brush and protector spray. Canvas shoes can often be machine washed on gentle cycle. Detailed care instructions are included with each pair.

Can I exchange my shoes for a different size?

+

Absolutely! We offer free size exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Contact our customer service team, and we'll send you the new size right away. You'll receive a prepaid label to return the original pair.

Do you have a loyalty program?

+

Yes! Our Shoe Lovers Rewards program gives you 1 point for every dollar spent. Earn exclusive discounts, early access to sales, and special birthday rewards. Sign up is free and you'll receive 50 bonus points just for joining!

Can I machine wash my shoes?

+

We do not recommend machine washing as it may damage the material. Instead, use a soft cloth or brush for cleaning.

How should I store my shoes??

+

Keep shoes in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. Use shoe trees or stuff them with paper to maintain shape.
